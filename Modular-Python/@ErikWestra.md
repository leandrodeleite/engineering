Erik Westra  
has been a professional software developer for over 25 years,  
and has worked almost exclusively in Python for the past decade.  

Erik's early interest in Graphical User Interface design  
led to the development of one of the most advanced urgent courier dispatch systems  
used by messenger and courier companies worldwide.  
In recent years,  
Erik has been involved in the design and implementation of systems  
matching seekers with providers of goods and services across a range of geographical areas,  
as well as real-time messaging, payment, and identity systems.  
This work has included the creation of real-time geocoders and map-based views  
of constantly changing data.  
Erik is based in New Zealand, and works for companies worldwide.
